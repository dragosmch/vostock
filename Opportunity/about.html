Why volunteer?

People choose to volunteer for a variety of reasons. For some it offers the chance to give something back to the community or make a difference to the people around them. For others it provides an opportunity to develop new skills or build on existing experience and knowledge. Regardless of the motivation, what unites them all is that they find it both challenging and rewarding.



“Volunteering offered me the opportunity to evolve both technically and socially. I have now discovered a new image of the world!”

“Your age does not matter. I encourage people of all ages to spend their time being a part of this great evolution.”



Where/How?

No matter if we talk about location or time, there are a multitude of opportunities out there. Several organisations are looking for volunteers and there is a variety of fields. Therefore, VoStock is the appropriate place to get organisations in touch with people who want to get involved. Just register, choose your interests and start looking for events!



“I have finally found a way to make a change in order to make the world we live in a better place.”

“If you have lots of free time, why not making it valuable?”
